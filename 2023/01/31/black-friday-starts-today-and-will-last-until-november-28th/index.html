Black Friday starts today and will last until November 28th

Posted on 31 Tháng Một, 2023 by admin

his year, Black Friday will take place on Friday, November 25th.
At Ledger, we have decided to spoil you, Black Friday starts today and will last until November 28th.

The need for a hardware wallet has become even more clear in the last week.

There has never been a better time to take control of your finances! Hardware wallets make self-custody easy and secure, so you can enjoy complete financial freedom with confidence. Forget relying on third parties to manage your funds – no one to tell you what to do or charge you fees. With Ledger, we can help make sure your money is, well, yours. Our cold wallets can assist you in taking the plunge so self-custody isn’t so scary.

The offer

This Black Friday is a special one. Starting now and lasting until Monday 28th, if you buy a Ledger Nano, you can get up to $30 of free BTC. Don’t think about it, get your BTC before it’s all gone.

  • Get $30 free BTC when you buy a Ledger Nano X.
  • Get $20 free BTC when you buy a Ledger Nano S Plus.

How does it work?

  1. Visit our Black Friday page, to purchase your new Ledger.
  2. Once you get your new Ledger, set it up with the Ledger Live app.
  3. Find a gift code on the physical card in your package and enter it in Ledger Live.
  4. 30 days after using your code, you’ll be able to redeem your free BTC!

Beginner in the crypto world? Get started with Nano S Plus and receive $20 free BTC. This is a perfect opportunity to get your first Bitcoin securely.

And if you’re already in the crypto world, or you prefer a Bluetooth connection? Try with Nano X.

You’ll get $30 free Bitcoin to make a Dollar Cost Average strategy on the most significant crypto in the ecosystem.

Still not sure which Ledger Nano to choose, find more information about our products on our comparison page.
